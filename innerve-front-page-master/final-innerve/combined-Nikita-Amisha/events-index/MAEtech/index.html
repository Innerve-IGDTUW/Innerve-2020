LINE FOLLOWING ROBOT

We will be providing the dimensions of the arena to the participants so that they can make the arena at home with the help of black tape and let their bots sense the black tape. The penalties, timing can be counted and judgement can be based on that.

Platform to be used:
1. Zoom app
2. Cisco webex

REALITY SOLVER

Teams will be asked to build devices, machines and robots which would help in solving problems from some given focus areas and then they have to present it and they will be judged on the basis of efficiency, reliability and flexibility of the prototype.

Platform to be used:
1. Zoom app
2. Cisco webex

×

MECHATHON

Just like the hackathons conducted by CSE/IT But in this the teams/individuals have to solve a mechanical related problem. For eg - Write a code for OTTO cycle simulator or simulating 4 bar mechanism and/animations. Team who writes the correct code in minimum time will be the winner.

Platform to conduct whole event:
1. Codechef
2. HakerRank

×

DIGITAL DOODLING

According to the theme provided one has to create the best out of waste like using the materials as newspaper, CDs , ice cream sticks or any other waste material which is easily available at home and is not new. Later on they have to explain their piece of art how it relates to the theme.

Platform to be used:
1. Zoom app
2. Cisco webex

×

HUNT IT OUT

Platform to be used:

1. Zoom app
2. Adobe Illustrator

AKRUTI

Just like treasure the participants have to answer the questions using some clues which are related to the field of mechanical once they answer the question they will get a pass code to move to the next question. The one who reaches the final destination first wins!

Platform to be used:
1. Google presentation
2. Google Form
3. Zoom app
